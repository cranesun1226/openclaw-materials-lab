# Research Report Template

## Research Goal

State the target use case and the question being answered.

## Evaluation Criteria

List the metrics or qualitative criteria used to compare candidates.

## Candidate Summary

For each candidate include:

- identifier,
- chemistry,
- core metrics,
- structural observations,
- notable strengths,
- notable risks.

## Ranking And Rationale

Explain the ranking in terms of the chosen criteria.

## Assumptions And Limitations

Document offline mode, missing data, failed paths, and uncertain interpretations.

## Artifacts

List saved structures, plots, notes, and related files.

## Next Steps

Suggest follow-up experiments, calculations, or validation work.
